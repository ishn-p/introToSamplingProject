# introToSamplingProject
Project files for intro to sampling

## Sampling Design
1. Simple random sample
- A simple random sample of n=200
2. Stratified sample:
- A simple random sample with n=100 for female and n=100 for male
3. One stage cluster sample, SRS
- A simple random sample with n=12 clusters and then all athletes in the clusters
4. One stage cluster sample, PPS
- A random PPS sample with n=12 clusters and then all athletes in the clusters
- The clusters could be different than SRS clusters
5. Cluster combined with stratification
- Within female stratum and male stratum, a simple random sample of 12 clusters and
then all athletes in the clusters
- The female and male strata should be independent, and could have different clusters
6. Two stage cluster sample, SRS
- First, a simple random sample of n=18 clusters
- Secondly a simple random sample of athletes in each cluster
  -  If Mi<=5, take mi=Mi-1
  - If 5<Mi<=20, take mi=Mi/2
  - If 20<Mi<=50, take mi=Mi/3
  -  If Mi>50, take mi=Mi/4
7. Two stage cluster sample, PPS
-  First, a random PPS sample of n=18 clusters, then a simple random sample of athletes
in each cluster, proportion of mi is same as SRS above
